This section captures the technical and operational gaps that **Tickify** aims to solve, presented in a high-impact visual format for the project report.

### Theoretical Foundation for the Visual
As illustrated above, modern event management faces four critical bottlenecks that justify the development of **Tickify**:

1.  **Manual Registration Latency**: Handling thousands of physical registration forms or disconnected digital links leads to data fragmentation and slow attendee onboarding.
2.  **Verification Bottlenecks**: Without real-time QR-based scanning, entry queues at large scale events become unmanageable, increasing the risk of ticket duplication and manual clerical errors.
3.  **Document/Certification Delays**: The traditional process of manually creating and emailing certificates to participants is labor-intensive and error-prone, requiring a specialized automated rendering engine.
4.  **Data Integrity Risks**: Security vulnerabilities in traditional paper-based or simple PDF tickets allow for unauthorized entry and easy forgery. Tickify addresses this through encrypted ticket generation and cloud-synced databases.
